Chris TessoneSignature Track· a month ago 
I believe JHU staff have posted elsewhere (not in this course's forum) that they are running the capstone three times a year. Keep in mind that they have to have a critical mass of students ready to take it, as we'll be working in teams and competing with other teams. They also have to get partners in industry and government on board so that the projects are real-world and compelling.

It's not realistic for JHU to offer the capstone as soon as every individual student is ready for it.

Hi, 

I hereby reporting an extract from a message that Brian, Jeff and Robert sent out to the april session's Data Scientist Toolbox students:

"The first offering of the Capstone session will not be available until October or November. There are two reasons for waiting until that time. First, we don't want to create an unhelpful incentive for students to rush through the course work. Second, we want to allow enough time for a sufficient number of students to qualify for participation. After our first Capstone session, we plan to offer Capstone opportunities every few months, so don't worry if you miss the first one; there will be other opportunities."
