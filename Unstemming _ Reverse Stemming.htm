    Maciej SzymkiewiczSignature Track· 2 days ago 

    Generally speaking stemming is not a reversible process. Just take following list of tokens:

    "run", "runner", "running", "runnable"

    If you use for example Lancaster stemmer all of these will be reduced to the stem "run". Other common stemmers may give different results but most of the time you loose some information.
